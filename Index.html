Welcome to Eloquent Countenance

Eloquent Countenance (교언영색) is a short 2.5D RPG Maker horror/narrative-driven game with visual novel/adventure elements following the story of a nun who was selected by the church to assist in progressing the pastor's funeral service suddenly hearing a strange voice who claims to be a "guardian angel". She must choose between following through with the instructions given to her or acting on her own.

More about the game!

Who are the games characters?

Forcas

Forcas seems to differentiate himself from the other angels in Heaven, being someone who always takes everything with a light heart and goofs around a lot. Despite having the ability to turn serious in a matter of seconds.

Angelica

She's someone who thinks clearly and through small hints that she's given. Also, some could say she is a generally curious woman who might try risky things to satisfy her interest, but that is also mostly up to the player.

PLOT:

The game introduces the player to a nun selected by the church to assist her pastor's sketchy funeral. As she goes about her way, she suddenly hears a strange voice in her head. It claims to be a guardian angel from Heaven, and she is skeptical at first, thinking that it's a demon. However, the unknown person makes himself visible and introduces himself, stating that his name is Forcas. He claims to want to guide the nun throughout this funeral, stating that she must keep to herself and not help anyone. The player can choose whether or not to follow this rule, and that will alter the nun's fate based on what she does.

 the game has MULTIPLE endings!

ENDING 1:

  • If the player ignores an unknown person locked inside a cage next to them, and tells the truth about one of the followers not having bought a Chrysanthemum, then the church will reveal to Angelica, the nun, that it was all a fake funeral to test if the pastor's wife was fully in control of the nun's body. This is because a possession ritual was performed on her during the wife's funeral to allow the deceased wife to come back to life through Angelica's vessel. However, according to Forcas, Angelica is still somehow conscious in her body alongside the wife, fighting for control. The church is now convinced that they don't have to do another ritual, and Forcas will from then on continue to try and salvage Angelica completely until she can defeat her possession.]

    • ENDING 2:

    • If the player helps the unknown person locked inside the cage next to them, and lies about everyone having their Chrysanthemums, despite Forcas' warnings about doing so, then the church will turn on her and think that the possession ritual on her did not work last time, because the pastor's wife "would never fall for cheap tricks like this" (helping the follower without a flower and letting out the person in the cage). The church prepares her for another possession ritual.]
